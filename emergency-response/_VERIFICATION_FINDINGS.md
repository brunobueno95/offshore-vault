## Confirmed findings

### Regulatory rename timeline

**Havtil replaced Petroleumstilsynet on 1 January 2024.** Official English name is "Norwegian Ocean Industry Authority" (Havindustritilsynet). Announced by Prime Minister Støre on 4 September 2023. Havtil sits under the Ministry of Labour and Social Inclusion. Primary source: `havtil.no/en/about-us/role-and-area-of-responsibility/our-history/`.

**Sokkeldirektoratet (Sodir) replaced Oljedirektoratet (NPD) on 1 January 2024.** English name: "Norwegian Offshore Directorate." Legal basis: Forskrift 19 December 2023 nr. 2181 (lovdata.no/forskrift/2023-12-19-2181). Primary source: `sodir.no/en/whats-new/news/general-news/2024/`.

**Norsk olje og gass rebranded to Offshore Norge in 2022.** NHO-affiliated landsforening. Scope now covers oil and gas, offshore wind, CCS, hydrogen, and seabed minerals. Primary source: `offshorenorge.no/om-oss`, cross-checked at `nho.no/landsforeninger`.

### Helicopter Safety Study

**HSS-4 (January 2023) is the current Helicopter Safety Study, not HSS-3b.** Published by SINTEF Digital, commissioned by Offshore Norge, covering the period 2010-2020 with forward outlook. HSS-3b (2017) is superseded and may be referenced only for historical trend analysis. Primary source: `sintef.no/globalassets/sintef-digital/report-hss-4-en---final-2023-01-17.pdf`.

### Offshore Norge guideline numbering (corrected)

The task originally had some mislabels. Confirmed numbering as of April 2026:

- **066** is the current helicopter operations guideline (rev 31 March 2025), NOT 064.
- **064** is "Etablering av områdeberedskap" (2015).
- **090** is "Common model for Safe Job Analysis" (2017), NOT competence.
- **002** is Safety and emergency preparedness training (rev 24, 20 June 2022).
- **074** is Helideck Manual (rev 04, July 2025, amended December 2025).
- **088** is Common model for work permits (rev 6, 2018).
- **096** is Man-overboard preparedness.
- **113** is Fallsikring og fallredning (20 October 2020).

Primary source: `offshorenorge.no/en/faginnhold/guidelines/guidelines/` guideline register.

### NORSOK editions in force

- **S-002 Edition 5** (2018) with AC:2021 corrigendum, Working environment.
- **S-003 Edition 4** (2017), Environmental care. Revision prNORSOK S-003 is out for public enquiry to 21 April 2026.

Primary source: `standard.no` and `store.accuristech.com`.

### RNNP 2024

RNNP 2024 (published 2025) shows:

- Offshore major-accident indicator is stable but up slightly versus 2023.
- Onshore indicators mostly stable.
- Maintenance backlog is a negative trend.
- Structural-damage incidents rose to nine in 2024, from three in 2023.

Primary source: `havtil.no` RNNP 2024 summary report.

### State SAR system

330 Skvadron RNoAF flies Leonardo AW101 "SAR Queen" at Sola, Ørland, Banak, Bodø, Rygge, and Florø. Tromsø is still civilian CHC S-92 until the planned 2030 handover.

Primary source: `regjeringen.no/en/topics/samfunnssikkerhet-og-beredskap/innsikt/nawsarh/`.

### Helicopter types on NCS 2026

- **Sikorsky S-92** is the dominant passenger type.
- **Leonardo AW189** is phasing in (CHC, Lufttransport).
- **Leonardo AW139** is used for Troll-Oseberg SAR.
- **Bell 525** is on order for Equinor, first four expected in 2026.
- **H175** is NOT in NCS service.
- **H225 / AS332L2** remain absent from NCS passenger service since the Turøy accident on 29 April 2016, despite the regulatory ban lift in 2017.

Primary sources: `equinor.com/news`, `tu.no`, `offshore-mag.com`, `energyvoice.com`.

### GSK training structure

- **Standard GSK:** 4 days. E-learning route shortens to 3 days.
- **GSK-repetisjon:** 2 days every 48 months.
- **Full re-sit:** required after 8 years.
- **Approved providers:** RelyOn Nutec, Falck Safety Services Norge, Maersk Training, ResQ, Stavanger Offshore Tekniske Skole (SOTS), NOSEFO, Kompetansebedriften.
- **OPITO BOSIET/FOET/TBOSIET** accepted under Mutual Agreement if CAT B rebreather EBS module and escape-chute element are included.
- **From 1 April 2026**, CAT B EBS training is mandatory for all NCS helicopter passenger transport.

Primary sources: `offshorenorge.no/temaer/kompetanse-og-utdanning/opplaring/`, `akerbp.com/en/traveling-offshore/`, `okea.no/travel-offshore/`.

### Helideck regulation stack

- **BSL D 5-1** (Forskrift 14 May 2019 nr. 604) under Luftfartstilsynet.
- Read together with **NORSOK C-004** and **CAP 437**.
- **Helideck Certification Agency** performs inspections.
- **DIFFS** standard for foam firefighting.

Primary sources: `luftfartstilsynet.no`, `firenor.no/systems`.

## Items to re-verify quarterly

Each of the following is a moving target or a recent change. Agents who notice a source conflict at write time must flag the relevant leaves and note the finding in this section. Do not edit the whitelist in CLAUDE.md without a human review.

- **330 Skvadron base operations.** Florø is operational 2024. Tromsø civilian-to-state transition is planned for 2030. Track any acceleration or delay.
